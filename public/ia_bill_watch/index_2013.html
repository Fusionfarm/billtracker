- The political reporting teams of The Gazette and Lee Enterprise's Iowa newspapers have teamed up to follow the most important pieces of legislation and will be tracking the progress of the proposals as they wind through the Iowa Legislature this session. The team will annotate important developments and translate some of the legi-speak into easy-to-understand updates.
- To use the application, select a category below or bill number. Select a bill to view additional details, follow its progress and noteworthy developments and see insights from our political reporting team.
- If you've got ideas of bills we should be following or want to suggest improvements for the app, e-mail us. Also, view our bill tracker for the 2014 session.
